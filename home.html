Our proyect

The service your logistics company needs

Our API service "SmartRoute" is the product of the current need in the market for platforms that offer logistics transport services, such as the Quick On Demand application, which informed us about their difficulties in accurately calculating the travel costs of a cargo vehicle. So our team took on the challenge of creating a rest API that calculates the approximate costs when taking a trip. For example: if the service originates in Bogot√° and ends in Cartagena, the value of the route must be charged to the system, including the following costs: tolls, gasoline value, vehicle wear and tear, etc. Our team identifies itself by assuming challenges that provide added value that benefits and facilitates the work of people or companies that require a technological solution.

What does our service offer?

Route calculation

We use an external API to find the best route to the destination and use this information to give a better result when calculating this expense

Toll calculation

The service uses a robust algorithm for toll search and location of these tolls on the route, thus making possible the delivery of a total of expenses in tolls

Calculation of vehicle expenses

Our algorithm is based on the calculation of the vehicles and their use our general formula to find those expenses

Safety of services

use SSL for make secure the API, limit request per minute to keep the flow controlled. and the use of token to connect and make requests through the token
